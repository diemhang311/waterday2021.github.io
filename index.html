The Story of Water Day

The best summer event in your life
Known as a festival that simulates the main activity of Songkran Festival - a festival to celebrate the Thai new year, Water Day has become a unique culture, an activity for many years. This outstanding cultural exchange activity are eagerly awaited by a large number of FPT University staff and students every summer.
  1. Water Day 2015: Té Nó
    Organized by Color Team
  2. Water Day 2016: Poderoso
    Organized by FU Student Council
  3. Water Day 2017: Poseidon
    Organized by Color Team
  4. Water Day 2018 - Fraicheur
    Organized by FU Student Council
  5. Water Day 2019 - ATHENA - The Last Battle
    Organized by Color Team
